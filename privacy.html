PowerHit Privacy Policy (Development)

Last updated: July 30, 2025

This app (PowerHit) is currently in development. The purpose of this app is to explore functionality related to coach-trainee fitness management, and is not yet available for public use.

What Data We Collect

All authentication data is securely managed by Amazon Cognito.
